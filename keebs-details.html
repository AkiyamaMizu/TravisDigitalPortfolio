Polytechnic Foundation Programme (Year 0)

FragranceKeebs

FragranceKeebs was a milestone project for me. Developed during my Year 0 Polytechnic Foundation Programme, this group project marked the very first time I implemented HTML and CSS basics into a fully functioning website.

While "Fragrance" is simply the company name, the project itself was a deep dive into building a structured web presence from scratch. It taught me the fundamentals of layout, styling, and collaboration that would become the foundation for all my future development work.

Our Story & Vision

This section illustrates the "Our Story" page, which serves as the narrative backbone of the company. It details the startup journey, explaining the "Why" behind our brand and how we began this venture.

Below the origin story, I implemented the "Our Vision" and "Our Mission" blocks. These sections are critical for establishing brand identity. I used clear headers and paragraph spacing to ensure that our core values—innovation and quality—were communicated effectively to the user. This was my first real experience in using typography to create a hierarchy of information.

Who Are We?

The "Who Are We?" section is a dedicated team showcase. This part of the site was designed to humanize the brand by displaying our team members' photos along with their names and specific roles, such as CEO.

I structured this using a grid layout to keep the profiles aligned. Each member card includes a personal quote to add personality, as well as their social media handles and email addresses. This taught me how to structure repetitive content elements (like profile cards) efficiently using CSS classes.
